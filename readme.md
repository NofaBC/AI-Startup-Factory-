## Features

- **12 Integrated Modules** – from Idea Intake to Investor Center and Startup Blueprint
- **Executive Dashboard** – real‑time readiness scores, progress tracking, and radar charts
- **Opportunity Validator** – scores your idea across 8 dimensions with strengths/weaknesses and AI recommendations
- **Market Analysis** – target personas, TAM/SAM/SOM, competitive landscape, and SWOT
- **Business Model Canvas** – value proposition, revenue streams, cost structure, and key partners
- **Product Architect** – core features, user journeys, MVP definition, and success metrics
- **Prototype Builder** – generates a build‑ready specification with tech stack, APIs, and deployment instructions
- **Brand Studio** – company name, tagline, mission, brand voice, color palette, and domain suggestions
- **Pricing Simulator** – interactive sliders for price, customers, growth, churn; live MRR/ARR/break‑even charts
- **Launch Planner** – checklists, product hunt plan, 90‑day roadmap, and beta program
- **Investor Center** – executive summary, pitch summary, financial projections, and funding ask
- **Startup Blueprint** – printable report that compiles all modules into one comprehensive document
- **AI Startup Advisor** – floating chatbot that answers questions using your current page data
- **Demo Startup** – pre‑filled “SmartClinic AI” example to illustrate the flow
- **Local Storage** – all data is saved automatically in your browser; no account required

## Target Users

- **Entrepreneurs** – validate and refine startup ideas
- **Product Managers** – structure product requirements and roadmaps
- **Founders** – prepare investor pitches and launch plans
- **Innovation Teams** – rapidly prototype new business concepts
- **Educators & Students** – teach lean startup methodology in a practical way

---

## Technology

- **HTML5 / CSS3 / JavaScript** (ES6)
- **React 18** (via CDN) – UI component model
- **Tailwind CSS** – utility‑first styling (light theme only)
- **Chart.js** – interactive radar and line charts
- **Lucide Icons** – clean, consistent SVG icons
- **Local Storage** – all data persistence
- **No backend, no database, no API keys** – runs 100% client‑side
